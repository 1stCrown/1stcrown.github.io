BRONYA ZAYCHIK

HERRSCHER OF TRUTH

Her Story

Orphan ➝Soldier ➝ Seeker ➝ Herrscher ➝ Guide of Humanity

Born in Siberia, Bronya lost her parents during the Second Honkai War and was taken in by Cocolia's orphanage, which secretly trained child soldiers.

Trained as an assassin, she earned the title "Silver Wolf of the Urals" due to her exceptional skills.

After forming a deep bond with fellow orphan Seele, Bronya sought to understand the world and her purpose, leading her to enroll at St. Freya Academy.

Bronya inherited the Herrscher of Reason core from Welt Yang, gaining the ability to construct objects and striving to use this power to save Seele.

As the Herrscher of Truth, she embraced humanity's capacity for change, guiding others with wisdom and compassion.

“The only inevitable truth about humanity is that humanity’s truth will change inevitably.”
